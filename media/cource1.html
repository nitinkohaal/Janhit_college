frequently asked questions

What is Amity University Online?

+

Amity University Online is the online learning platform of Amity University, one of India's leading private universities. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs across various disciplines, delivered entirely online.

What is the admission process of Amity Online?

+

The admission process for Amity Online involves selecting your preferred course, submitting the online application form along with necessary documents, and, upon acceptance, finalizing enrollment by fulfilling the fee payment requirements. It's a streamlined pathway to access quality online education through Amity Online's diverse range of programs.

How do I access my course?

+

After receiving the login credentials of LMS, enrolled students can start with the program. All the course-related information is structured in LMS, through which students can access their live sessions, study materials, assignments and more. Learners can access all the study material 24X7. So, you can study at your own pace and time at your convenience.

How will I get admission confirmation?

+

Once your admission process is completed and the enrollment number is generated, our support team will get in touch with you for admission confirmation.

Cancellation of admission

+

Cancellation of admission will be granted on the special approval from the competent authority on a case-to-case basis.

What academic support services are available to online students?

+

Amity University Online offers a range of academic support services, including academic advising and technical support, to help students succeed.

How are exams and assessments conducted in online programs?

+

Assessments may vary depending on the course but typically include a combination of quizzes, assignments, and projects. Exams are conducted through a remote online proctoring system and can be taken from anywhere.

Are student loans available for online students?

+

Yes, we offer various options, including scholarships, loans, and easy payment plans, to help make education more accessible for our students.

How are online classes conducted?

+

Online classes are conducted through live lectures, pre-recorded videos, interactive discussions, and virtual classrooms. Students can participate in real-time or access recordings later, providing flexibility to accommodate various schedules and time zones.
