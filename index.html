About Our University

Shaping the Future of Legal Excellence

Janhit College of Law is a premier institution under the esteemed Janhit Group of Institutions, committed to excellence in legal education and the development of ethical legal professionals.

01

Commitment to Excellence

Janhit College of Law offers a structured academic environment that blends legal theory with hands-on learning through moot courts, legal aid camps, internships, and research activities.

02

Experienced Leadership & Vision

Backed by the Janhit Institute of Education and Information, the college is guided by expert academicians and professionals dedicated to advancing justice and legal education.

03

Strategic Location & Modern Facilities

Located in Knowledge Park-I, Greater Noida, the campus features smart classrooms, a rich library, advanced IT labs, and is well-connected to major metro routes.

News and Events

From our students clinching global honors to the university soaring in national and international rankings,

Debate Competition on 'One Nation, One Election'

A debate competition was organized on the topic "One Nation, One Election," encouraging students to engage in critical discussions about electoral reforms in India. Winners were awarded for their insightful arguments and public speaking skills.

Legal Aid Centre Inaugurated on Legal Services Day

On the occasion of Legal Services Day, Janhit College of Law inaugurated a Legal Aid Centre in Greater Noida. This initiative aims to provide free legal assistance to those in need, reinforcing the college's commitment to social justice and community service.

Annual Sports Day Celebrated with Enthusiasm
